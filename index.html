About Us

Overview

Diamond Mind Executive is a multi-purpose organization in Information and Communication Technology. We have professionals and experts as workers in the different specializations of the organization.

Who We Are

Diamond Mind Executive is one of the vast growing organization in Nigeria soon to be in Africa. Our objective is to make processes and the information technology development clear and easy to every child and adults. We are committed to the growth of our people from the grassroot (Local Government - Ikot Ekpene) to the wide world.

Our Mission is implemented through our services, skill acquisition, products, convenings, partnership etc as we are committed to excellence, high level of professionalism, intensive/extensive growth and an organization filled with integrity. The organization leverages her reputation and growth on the recommendations of her patronizers, partners and members of the public and timely meet of client needs.

Why ICT

Information Technology is one confusing and bulky skill to acquire, sometimes it is underrated. But understanding the process of Information Technology and being able to communicate it rightly is an exciting and profiting skill everyone should have. It has the capacity to shift times 10 of your starting position. Starting from being a good internet mogul to an advertiser, to a marketer, to a media influencer, to a brand consultant and to a web controller/adviser. We believe that our focus on this aspect will spur the young generation to acquire these values as it will make them resourceful and men - women of impact in the society.

Impact

Since inception, Diamond Mind Executive has constantly put smile to the faces of her clients over a 100 of them as at this date (July 18, 2021). She has train people in offline computer knowledge and the various aspect of her specializations. People that have benefited from us are now growing in their different factions of life. Beyond our present impacts, Diamond Mind Executive continues to be of relevance to her patronizers and the general public.
